the problem

the cost of not having a system of record

when a program runs on disconnected tools and manual reporting, the gaps compound.

change orders get missed. cost overruns surface too late to contain. contract obligations fall through because no one owns the tracking. board presentations require a week of data assembly. and the people responsible for outcomes are always working from a picture that's already out of date.

aurigo closes those gaps. one platform, built specifically for owners, that holds the entire program together — contracts, costs, controls, compliance — updated in real time, accessible across the organization.

the outcome: program leaders who know where things stand before anyone has to ask.

the platform

what the platform delivers

01 / PROJECT CONTROLS
project controls

live schedule and risk visibility across every active project in the portfolio. flag problems when they're still manageable. stop building reports — start making decisions.

what owners gain: the full picture, in real time, without the weekly status call.
02 / CONTRACT MANAGEMENT
contract management

every contract, amendment, approval, and obligation tracked in a single auditable system. no more email-chain archaeology. no more missed milestones because the obligation was buried in a PDF.

what owners gain: procurement and legal oversight that scales with the program.
03 / COST TRACKING
cost tracking

program-level budget vs. actuals — not rolled-up project numbers that obscure exposure. see where the money is, where it's at risk, and where there's room to move before the quarter closes.

what owners gain: cost clarity that supports real decisions, not just financial reporting.
04 / PROGRAM VISIBILITY
program visibility

one source of truth for the entire portfolio. when leadership asks for a status update, the answer already exists in the system. no assembly required.

what owners gain: a platform that removes the week of prep before every board meeting.
